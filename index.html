Admission Information

We are currently accepting applications for the Fall 2025 semester.

Undergraduate Admission Requirements:

  • Minimum GPA 2.5 in both SSC & HSC or equivalent
  • Completed online application form
  • Photocopy of academic transcripts

Postgraduate Admission Requirements:

  • Minimum CGPA 2.5 in undergraduate program
  • Professional experience (for MBA – preferred)

Application Deadline: September 30, 2025

Information for International Students

Welcome to Jamuna University! Here's what you need to know if you're applying from outside Bangladesh.

Visa Requirements:

  • Offer letter from Jamuna University
  • Valid passport
  • Proof of financial support

Tuition Fees (per semester):

  • Undergraduate: $1,200
  • Postgraduate: $1,500

Registration Process:

  1. Complete the online application
  2. Submit academic documents and passport scan
  3. Receive acceptance letter
  4. Apply for student visa
